About me.

I lead marketing at Culdesac, an innovative Arizona-based developer building walkable, mixed-use neighborhoods. My job is translating what we're building into stories that resonate — running organic and paid campaigns, coordinating large-scale events, managing press visits from outlets like NYT, CNN, and The Daily Show, and building decks for high-profile speaking engagements.

Before Culdesac, I worked in the City of Phoenix Mayor's Office, and created public engagement materials on climate resilience for the City of Tempe.
